How I can help your company grow.

Who am I?

My name is Boyd Richards. I have been developing E-Learning Solutions for the last 5 years. Right now I am the Vice President of course Development for Enspark. We have built e-learning applications for clients such as: the State of Utah, Google, and the Federal Government. We also build off the shelve courses that can help companies overcome many of the obstacles they face including; Leadership training, Negotiation training, and Sexual Harassment training.

What I do

First and foremost I build custom courses that will fit your companies needs. With my team we have built courses on every imaginable topic. If you need to teach your company a skill or attribute we can help. We have extensive experience in getting those courses to to perform well on any platform, be it mobile, or desktop. We are compliant with all major learning management services, and if you need us to host the courses we have our in house LMS and hosting service as well.
